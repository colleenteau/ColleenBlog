Something About Me, Myself and I

Hi everyone, I’m Colleen Te Au. I was born in and returned to live in Gore, Southland. Don’t hold that against me haha! As a result I’m well travelled and have lived abroad. My background and skillset is in social work and community development. I value loyalty, honesty, empathy and kindness. I’m a mum to four pretty amazing kids and that keeps things real on my journey. Really real. After a nearly 20 year career in Social Services I felt it was now or never to jump on the tech train and pursue the line of work I had actually planned on doing. I’m a strong and determined person, the consummate team player and great to have in your corner when the chips are down. I’m attending EDA because I believe the skills I can learn here and networks will provide a great platform to open doors to creativity and opportunities. My dream is to be proficient enough in web development to work on my own ideas, travel and work remotely. I’m looking forward to living in Wellington for a couple of months, meeting you all, forging lifelong links and sharing on this adventure. Nga mihi nui
